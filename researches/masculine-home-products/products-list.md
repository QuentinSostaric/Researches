## 1. TEXTILES

### Plaid / Throw Blanket
**Source** : [Fast Ways to Create a Gentleman's Christmas Home](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p0Kf4gpGX28) - 01:00

**Description exacte du YouTuber** :
> "You're looking for **weight and natural fibers**. Wool, heavy cotton, cashmere blends, or alpaca. Eye-catching yet comfortable to the touch."

**Motifs recommandés** :
- Subtle herringbone
- Window pane plaid
- Fisherman's knit
- Heathered solids

**Couleurs** : Pine green, oxblood, espresso brown, camel, navy

**Placements suggérés** :
- Draped over the arm of a leather chair
- Folded cleanly on coffee table's lower shelf
- Layered across the back of a sofa
- Placed at the front of the bed

## 4. TAPIS

### Tapis peau de vache / Cowhide Rug
**Source** : [The Cowhide Rug... Done With Authority](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mXwN4yymQ9A) - 00:00

**Description exacte** :
> "If you want the room to feel distinguished and masculine, lean toward **rich browns, espresso tones, or deep charcoals**. Let the shape and texture carry visual interest, not a gimmicky print."

**À ÉVITER** :
- "Bold black and white spotted cowhide" (qualifié de "novelty", "rarely convey taste")
- Tapis trop petit ("A proper hide should spill out beyond the anchor points of your furniture")
- Ambiance "full cowboy" (wood planks, horseshoes, taxidermy)

**Conseils de style** :
- Peut être layered sur un tapis wool, jute ou sisal
- Laisser la forme guider le flow de la pièce
- "Peek from beneath a chair, arc beside a coffee table, extend past the edge of a bed"

## Notes importantes

1. **Pas de marques spécifiques** : Le YouTuber ne mentionne jamais de marques, seulement des caractéristiques et matériaux.

2. **Philosophie clé** : "Contrast is what gives a room presence" - le contraste foncé/clair est essentiel.

3. **À éviter absolument** :
   - "Whatever coffee table was on sale"
   - "Beige on beige, gray on gray"
   - "Bold black and white spotted cowhide"
   - "Sparkly, glittery, or ribbon covered" decor
   - "Excessively sweet" candles

4. **Température d'éclairage** : Toujours **2700 Kelvin** (warm)
